<section class="resource-intro">
  <p class="profile-kicker">Design System</p>
  <h2>Quiet, technical, editorial.</h2>
  <p>
    The site uses deep neutral surfaces, restrained blue and green accents, mono
    metadata, and comfortable reading rhythm.
  </p>
</section>

<div class="resource-grid">
  <section class="resource-card">
    <span>Palette</span>
    <h3>Dark Surfaces</h3>
    <p>Primary background, elevated cards, and thin borders create depth without visual noise.</p>
  </section>

  <section class="resource-card">
    <span>Typography</span>
    <h3>Inter + JetBrains Mono</h3>
    <p>Inter carries long-form reading. JetBrains Mono marks navigation, metadata, and technical labels.</p>
  </section>

  <section class="resource-card">
    <span>Cards</span>
    <h3>Editorial Notes</h3>
    <p>Cards use subtle lift, category accent rules, readable summaries, and compact topic chips.</p>
  </section>

  <section class="resource-card">
    <span>Motion</span>
    <h3>Calm Feedback</h3>
    <p>Motion is limited to gentle reveal, hover lift, progress indication, and back-to-top behavior.</p>
  </section>
</div>
